Biology Lab

This survey laboratory is designed to expose biological science students to basic principles of biology through experiments and other procedures to reinforce material that has been or will be covered in the associated lecture. The student will be able to perform the following: Develop an understanding of the process of science. Develop and understanding of laboratory safety procedures and the use of scientific instrumentation. Develop an understanding of the basic structure and function of the cell. Discuss the chemistry of life as related to organisms (both plant and animal). Be able to contrast living and non-living matter. Develop and understanding of cellular reproduction and human heredity as it relates to the diversity of life and human traits.

Jazz History

Jazz History is a course that traces the development of Jazz music from the turn of the century to the present. The primary focus is the historically significant musicians who played an important role in developing the many facets of Jazz music

American History

This is a lecture-based course that requires reading and completing assignments outside of the classroom. In this course we will cover Reconstruction era and then go through the eras of industrialization, progressivism, the two World Wars to the present

Ubiquitous Handheld

This course is an introduction to Conversational AI, commonly known as chat bots. We will use the Google Platform DialogFlow to design, build and deploy Cognitive Digital Assistants.
